#17 — Carousel — Transaction Flow
Credit Cards 201
Transaction flow
Every credit card transaction is a multi‑step process involving the cardholder, merchant, network (Visa), issuing bank (Lead), and acquiring bank.
Purchase
Swipe
Terminal contacts Visa to request authorization from issuing bank
Approval
Authorize
Bank checks credit & fraud, approves the transaction, merchant completes sale
Settlement
Settle
Issuing bank sends funds (minus interchange) through Visa to merchant's bank
Billing
Statement
Monthly statement with total balance, minimum payment, and due date
